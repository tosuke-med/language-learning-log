I started my language learning log again.

This kind of regular output is very important for language learning because it helps activate my English speaking skills every day. Once they’re activated, I continue using English words to understand things or to talk about topics, even when I’m speaking my native language.

Having regular practice in the morning is a good habit for improving my English. For example, I do laundry every day, and during that time I can talk freely to myself about anything. It’s a good opportunity to practice. Combining this kind of practice with a daily routine is a very effective method.
