Motivation is one of the most important factors in language learning. I am going to work in the U.S. for several months. This opportunity gives me much stronger motivation to learn English than before.

For a long time, I continued studying English, but I did not actually have a clear motivation because I had no specific purpose. This opportunity may change my learning environment.

I do not like repetitive learning methods such as Anki. However, if I have a clear purpose, I can tolerate this sometimes boring approach. I will try to remember useful phrases and sometimes write them here.

Today I learned the phrase “do an impression of.”
This means to imitate someone.

For example:
“I do an impression of my mentor. I sometimes tell stories with a pinch of salt.”
