Last week I had a terrible cold, and I didn't feel like doing anything. Fortunately, I recovered two days ago, but I couldn't update my language learning log during that time.

I've just started a bootcamp-style study plan for French, and now that I have resumed it, I also started trying this bootcamp plan for English.

Usually, I learn and collect phrases from news sites, novels, books, or anything I'm interested in. Then, after one or two weeks of collecting these phrases and words, I review all of them for one week in a very intensive and focused period.

I will try this style for a while and then review its effectiveness.
