title: Talking with a child in English
date: 2026-04-05
language: en
tags: [all]
---

When I went to an amusement park, I talked with a boy from Australia. He looked about seven or eight years old, so I needed to choose words and topics that were appropriate for his age. However, I sometimes found it difficult to quickly find the right words.

I usually feel comfortable when I talk about my research or other professional topics, so this was an unexpected experience for me.

I think this happened because familiarity with a situation plays a very important role in speaking a second language. If you are not used to a situation, it can be quite difficult to respond appropriately.
