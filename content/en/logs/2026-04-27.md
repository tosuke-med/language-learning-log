Yesterday I had dinner with international students at a welcoming party. I found it was sometimes difficult to describe local foods to foreigners.

I learned some expressions from the experience yesterday.

For example, tofu is bland or mild, so you need salt with it.

Some people say natto is stinky, but I'm surprised you didn't feel that.

Liver is a little bit chewy and bloody.

Actually, it was fun to see people eat something new for the first time. I enjoyed seeing their reactions.
