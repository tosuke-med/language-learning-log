---
title: Respect for scientists who go abroad
date: 2026-04-07
language: en
tags: [listening]
---

I started cell experiments yesterday, and I realized something.
Some scientists go abroad to learn new methods or to achieve better results and publish in major journals. I now feel that these kinds of complex and abstract techniques are very difficult to communicate in English.

Even in Japanese, it is sometimes hard to understand what a mentor is saying when they explain a method or how to use certain instruments. In English, it becomes even more difficult, and it is hard to communicate properly, especially when learning how to use these instruments.

Overall, I really feel a strong sense of respect. I respect people who go abroad to learn these complex and abstract experimental techniques.
