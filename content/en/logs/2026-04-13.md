The book I read also provides useful advice on learning from difficult materials. I usually read books in English that are somewhat challenging to understand. However, easier materials can become boring if I try to read them repeatedly. Instead, even if the content is difficult, reading multiple books on the same topic or within the same context can help reinforce the same words and phrases through repeated exposure. This seems like a very helpful approach for me.
