Recently, my motivation to study languages has been declining. I still need English for reading research articles, books, and for work, but I really don’t feel like doing it. I think the main reason is that I am waiting for the result of the research program I applied to.

I feel that motivation is like a wave. It is difficult to keep it constant, so I need to ride it like a surfer. During low periods, I don’t feel like doing anything related to language learning, even enjoyable activities such as watching dramas like Fallout (which I actually like).

So, for now, I focus on reviewing sentences using quizzes created by an LLM and simply wait for my motivation to come back.
