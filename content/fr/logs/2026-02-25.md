I started to feel a bit bored learning new words and phrases by watching beginner French videos. It does not mean that I have already mastered all the A1-level phrases well, but I think this feeling is an important signal from my brain. So I changed my approach and began a two weeks “bootcamp” plan.

I usually do not like memorizing words and phrases repeatedly like in school. However, this method can sometimes be useful for short-term understanding.

I asked ChatGPT for essential phrases and added them to the phrase list I had already learned. I attached the phrase list at the end of this page.

After I master these phrases, almost like a game, I will go back to videos or news sites that interest me. I feel that sometimes this kind of leap is necessary.
