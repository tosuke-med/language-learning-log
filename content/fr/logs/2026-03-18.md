YouTube videos are very useful learning materials for language learning because they use natural expressions and pronunciation in real-life settings. However, I always feel a barrier when learning this way, since the language level I can enjoy is often above my current level, especially at the beginning.

Still, interesting content is very attractive for learning. So, I use this prompt to get used to the expressions used in the video.

Prompt example
```
Transform the following YouTube video transcript into CEFR A1-level French. If some words are above A1 level, keep only those that are essential and commonly used to preserve the meaning, and provide a word list for them. Do not shorten or omit any information; retain the full content of each sentence.
```

I use this with YouTube transcripts and read the output first. After that, I watch the video. This method really helps me understand words and phrases in natural settings and improves my listening.

I lost motivation recently due to my health condition and business, but I have managed to regain my motivation using this method.
