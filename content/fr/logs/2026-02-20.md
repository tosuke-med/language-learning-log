When I lose motivation to study languages, it becomes much harder to restart French because it does not directly help my work. Therefore, when my motivation drops, I set a very easy goal for French learning, such as reading just one page or reviewing a short quiz and considering that enough for the day.

So, I just only learn this phrase.

Où est le quai?
(Where is the platform?)

It's very practical when you use a train or metro.

That's really all for today.
